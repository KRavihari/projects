Patna

Patna is a wonderful place in the middle of a tea estate in the Pallegama area of Deniyaya. Due to the biodiversity and beauty of the Deniyaya area, this area has become very popular among tourists.So Patna is a place where you can slip on the water because of this. Also known as brush stone. Patna also has a wonderful landscape. There is a large boulder here that is slightly slippery and slippery. It feels like it's slipping down a seso. Due to the biodiversity and beauty of the Deniyaya area, this area has become very popular among tourists. Patna attracts a large number of tourists to experience this wonderful natural experience. Naturally created by a stream flowing down the Gin River, this place can be enjoyed by a large number of people at once and you can see several waterfalls while walking downhill from here. You can climb the rock and slide along the rock with the group and have fun with the water pipes. Needless to say, this beautiful place is now getting ugly due to some activities of tourists who do not care about nature. Therefore, tourists should make sure to have fun here without harming the environment.
